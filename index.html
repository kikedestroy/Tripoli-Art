Galería is a contemporary Art Gallery based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, founded in 2016 by Eugenio C. Ottolenghi and it is specialized in Latin American art.

Galería opens its doors in Buenos Aires in 2016, with artists who work with contemporary features but attached to traditional disciplines such as painting, sculpture, collage and photography. Our artists are rooted on clearly defined genres such as abstraction, landscape, still life and lyrical abstraction. We are focused on the dealing and promotion of contemporary Latin American art, and we also offer works from the secondary market by other contemporary and modern artists. We aim to have a balance between newcomers, mid-career and established artists.
