STATMENT

Painting for me is allowing me to be myself. I started doing it because I love it, it takes me on a journey without time and without limits.
Through painting I managed to unlearn everything that makes me a person that I am not and connect with my most intimate emotions. Art is the daily imprint that I leave on the canvas.

The process begins with an idea and becomes practical when I prepare the frame, the paints and the materials. I perceive what I feel or what others transmit to me and the canvas gets flooded with that feeling. Color is a vehicle that conveys my state of mind and paint strokes are my impulses. Each work is the result of the ability to interpret what the world offers me and turn it into something new.

MARIA LAURA TRIPOLI
